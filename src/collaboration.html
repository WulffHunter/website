Collaboration

Papers

I'm currently an author on 2 papers: one for the Journal of Language and Memory, and another for the International Conference of Software Engineering (ICSE).

For the paper for the Journal of Language and Memory, I created both the PRE Algorithm and the Rational TE Algorithm to analyze transpositional errors in number recall.

For the ICSE paper, I worked with Professor Andriy Miranskyy to create some Recurrent Neural Network models using Keras and TensorFlow to analyze log strings.

To learn more about these papers, reach out to me!

I built and wrote a shader lab for Ryerson's Virtual Reality course, in which students learn the fundamentals of Unity's shaders and experiment with simple shader examples.

The lab will be available to students starting in the Winter 2021 semester at Ryerson.

Clubs

I've been a part of two fantastic clubs at Ryerson: the Association of Ryerson Role-players and Gamers (ARRG!) and the Ryerson AI team.

With ARRG!, I helped create the ARRG D&D group, which coordinates regular Dungeons& Dragons games for Ryerson students and pairs players up with Game Masters. I later became the Vice-President and President of the club, where we managed to recruit over 100 new players.

With the Ryerson AI team, I worked with the Engineering department to create a Ryerson AI mobile app, which is hopefully coming soon!

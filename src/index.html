About me

Hi there! I'm Jared Rand. Welcome to my website.

I've written a novel, dabbled in stand-up (I've got a video of it somewhere on YouTube), and I'm obsessed with finding good music.

Reach out to me if you want to know more, or even just to chat.

Have fun checking out my website!

My goal with this website was to introduce people to who I am and showcase some of my skills. I hope this website, and the game above, accomplish that, or at least let you have a bit of fun while looking me up.
